About us


Welcome to ANN's Restaurant, where flavor, tradition, and hospitality come together to create an unforgettable fancy dinning experience.
Nested in the heart of Bunamwaya, Our restaurant is a celebration of culinaryheritage and mordern taste, bringing you the finest ingredients and seasonal flavours.

Our story began with passion for authentic Itallian, indian and local recipes. Each dish is crafted with care, from time-honored family recipes to inventive creations that show case the essence of each ingredient. at Anns Restaurant, we believe food is more than just a meal; its an experience meant to be shared.

We're commited to sourcing locally and sustainably, partnering with farmers, fishermen, and artisans who share our dedication to quality. every meal served reflects our values of freshness, authenticity, and respect for the craft.

Our warm welcoming atmosphere is designed to make you feel at home. Wheather you're joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, our team is here to provide a fancy dinning experience that's as memorable as it's deliciousness.

Come in, relax, and enjoy the flavors we've brought together for you. At Anns Restaurant, you're not just a guest but you're part of our family.
